Software

The SkySense software is responsible for managing sensor data acquisition, communication with the ground station and data processing.
The system is programmed in Python and runs on a Raspberry Pi Pico, which collects environmental data such as altitude, pressure, temperature, acceleration and air quality. Data packets are transmitted to the ground station at a frequency of at least 1 Hz, while also being stored locally on an SD card to prevent data loss in case of transmission issues.
A key innovative feature of our software is the data-to-music conversion system, which transforms scientific measurements into musical parameters such as pitch, rhythm and tempo. The generated sound is further refined using AI-based music generation tools, creating a unique combination of scientific data and artistic expression.

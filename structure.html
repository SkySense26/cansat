Structure

The structure of SkySense is designed to be compact, lightweight and robust, ensuring both mechanical resistance and efficient use of internal space. The shell is produced with PLA, a lightweight and environmentally friendly material derived from renewable sources. The internal layout is carefully designed to organize electronic components efficiently while allowing quick access for maintenance and testing. Openings in the outer structure allow the internal environment of the CanSat to balance with the surrounding atmosphere, ensuring accurate measurements of pressure and temperature. The structure also guarantees proper exposure of the air quality sensors to the external environment.
